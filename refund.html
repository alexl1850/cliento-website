Refund Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

We want you to love Akus. If you don't, we'll make it right — no awkward conversations, no hoops to jump through.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with Akus within the first 30 days of your first paid subscription, email us at support@akus.com.au with "Refund Request" in the subject line and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

This applies to your first payment only. It does not apply to subsequent monthly renewals.

Free Trial

Akus offers a 7-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial period. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged at all. No refund is needed — there's nothing to refund.

Cancellation

You can cancel your Akus subscription at any time from your account settings. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will not be charged again after cancellation
  • We do not refund the remaining days in your current billing period
  • Your account data is retained for 30 days after cancellation before deletion

Exceptions

We may decline a refund in cases where we determine the service has been misused, including but not limited to generating spam, violating our Terms of Service, or creating multiple accounts to obtain multiple free trials.

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and will be returned to your original payment method.

Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under the Australian Consumer Law. If our service has a major failure, you are entitled to a refund under the ACL regardless of this policy.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@akus.com.au with:

  • Subject line: "Refund Request"
  • Your account email address
  • Reason for the refund (optional but helpful)

We'll respond within 1 business day.
