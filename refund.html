Refund Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Plain English: Not happy in your first 30 days? Email us and we'll give you your money back. No fuss, no forms, no questions. We want to earn your trust.

1. Our 30-Day Guarantee

If you sign up for Cliento Pro and aren't satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund your first monthly payment in full — no questions asked. Just email support@cliento.com.au with the subject "Refund Request" and include the email address on your account. We'll process your refund within 5 business days.

2. Free Trial

Nothing is charged during the 7-day free trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged anything at all.

3. After the First 30 Days

After the initial 30-day guarantee period, we don't offer refunds on monthly payments that have already been charged — except if: the service was unavailable for more than 24 hours in a row; you were charged by mistake; or you were charged twice for the same period. If any of these happen, please get in touch and we'll fix it.

4. Cancelling

You can cancel at any time from your account settings, or by emailing us. You'll keep access until the end of the month you've already paid for. We don't give partial refunds for the days you have left in a billing period.

5. Your Rights Under Australian Law

This policy doesn't take away any rights you have under the Australian Consumer Law. If there's a major failure with our service, you're entitled to a remedy regardless of what this policy says.

6. How Refunds Are Paid

Refunds go back to the card you used to pay, usually within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

7. Contact Us

Email: support@cliento.com.au · We respond: Within 1 business day, Monday to Friday AEST
