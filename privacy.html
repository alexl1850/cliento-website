Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Plain English: We only collect what we need to run your account. We never sell your information to anyone. You can ask us to delete everything at any time. We follow the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

1. Who This Applies To

AKUS Digital Pty Ltd operates Cliento at cliento.com.au. This policy explains how we look after your personal information under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles.

2. What Information We Collect

Account details: Your name, email address, and password (stored securely — we can't read your password) when you sign up.
Business information: Your business name, what you do, your suburb, and a description — used only to create your marketing content.
Payment information: We never see or store your card details. All payments go through Paddle directly.
Usage information: How you use the platform, your IP address, browser type — used to improve Cliento.

3. How We Use Your Information

To create your account, provide the service, process payments, send you account-related emails, and improve Cliento. We only send marketing emails with your permission and you can unsubscribe any time.

4. Who We Share It With

We never sell your information. We only share what's necessary to run the service — with Anthropic (the AI that powers the content), Paddle (who handles payments), and our hosting provider. All of them are required to protect your data.

5. How We Keep It Safe

Your information is stored on secure servers, encrypted when sent over the internet (HTTPS), and encrypted when stored. If a serious data breach ever occurs, we will tell you and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You can ask us to show you, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Email privacy@cliento.com.au and we'll respond within 30 days. If you're not happy with how we've handled things, you can also complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

7. How Long We Keep It

We keep your information while your account is active and for up to 7 years after you close it, as required by law.

8. International Transfers

Some of our providers — including Anthropic, who powers the AI — operate outside Australia. We take reasonable steps to make sure your information is protected to the same standard required under Australian law.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@cliento.com.au · OAIC complaints: oaic.gov.au
