The honest take

Where an agency is still right

If you're about to raise a Series A, rebrand a 30-year-old business, or need someone to walk into a boardroom and defend a campaign strategy — hire an agency. That's genuinely not what Akus does, and any product that claims otherwise is overselling itself.

What most local businesses actually need month to month isn't strategy — it's consistent execution: a website that doesn't rot, social posts that go out on schedule, reviews that get responded to, and blog content that keeps showing up in Google. That's the part agencies charge $2,000+/month to do manually, and it's exactly what Akus automates for $50.
